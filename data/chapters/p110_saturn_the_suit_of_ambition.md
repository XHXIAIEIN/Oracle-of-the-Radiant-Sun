# Saturn — The Suit of Ambition

> *Suit title page (p110).* The Saturn glyph (♄︎) above the title "SATURN — The Suit of Ambition", with an illustration of an old, scythe-bearing figure driving a chariot drawn by a serpent; the signs of Aquarius (♒︎) and Capricorn (♑︎) — both ruled by Saturn — are shown on the chariot wheels.

By interpreting the combinations of planets and signs revealed by this suit, you can explore how the subject of a reading may advance materially and socially. Saturn represents responsibilities and the boundaries and structure that enable society to function. This planet is traditionally associated with restrictions and obstacles. It has a reputation for being a limiting factor in a reading, but only because it indicates areas in life which the questioner must control and apply great patience and determination to. Saturn also represents the ability to apply oneself to a project with persistence and the determination to achieve aims. Saturn is the natural ruler of the Tenth House, which refers to one's professional life, honor, fame and worldly success. Thus, the sign which Saturn occupies denotes the best route to success, and also the difficulties that may be encountered on the way.

*Saturn occupations:* Hoteliers, bartenders; bricklayers, laborers; magicians; secretaries; shopkeepers; miners and gardeners.

*Saturn affinities:* Saturday; lead, jet; black; the bones, teeth, knees; beetles, nocturnal birds, crows, crocodiles, goats; farming; rubbish; grandfathers; narcotics; old buildings, prison.
