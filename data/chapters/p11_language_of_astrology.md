# The Language of Astrology

Astrology is a language. Each zodiac sign, planet and House of a horoscope in combination builds the letters, words and phrases that enable astrologers to interpret the meaning of any given moment. Each astrological House is naturally ruled by a sign, and each sign is governed by one or two planets. Although the Sun and the Moon are not planets, for the sake of simplicity, they are referred to as such. The table on page 12 represents the twelve divisions of the zodiac, and each one features key words. The words next to the House describe realms of life experience; the words following the planets describe functions or actions; and the definitions by the signs set out the nature or style of an action. By combining the host of different combinations and permutations of the three qualities of House, sign and planet, we can develop the most complex descriptions of possibilities in any situation. The weekly snippets in magazine sun-sign horoscopes are very basic compared to this, and have only the vaguest relationship to astrology proper.
