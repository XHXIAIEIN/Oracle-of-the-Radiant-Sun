# Using the Cards

Although there are many ways to use the Radiant Sun Oracle cards, the two most useful readings are the Sun Year and the Horary methods. Both of these are based on the circle of the Sun. The Sun Year gives a month-by-month description of the year ahead, while the Horary offers answers to specific questions by looking at the cards occupying the houses that relate to the questions.

We will start with instructions for a Sun Year reading, which takes as its logical base the calendar. You can feel confident with this method for its ability to answer both specific enquiries and more general life questions. Start by completing a reading for the twelve cards of the year; you can then zoom in on any particular month, week or day, or even go further and look at a particular hour of the day, perhaps, to illuminate an important meeting or work task.

We then present step-by-step instructions for a Horary reading. In this type of reading, you ask a specific question, and it should be possible to make a yes or no answer. Questions can range from life's big worries – Will I sell my house? Will he propose marriage? – to the seemingly trivial – Will I find my keys? If you study the subjects identified with each House position, and combine this with knowledge of the actions described by the seven planets and the nature of the signs, you will soon be using the various combinations to predict the outcome of any reading. It takes time to study all the variables, but once learnt, this method becomes astonishingly accurate as a way to help you make difficult decisions or just to give quick answers to questions that have aroused your curiosity.

Finally, we introduce two further methods of making a reading with the Sun oracle cards: The Grand Trine and The Cardinal Square.
