# Qualities associated with the Houses, planets and signs

| House / Realm of experience | Planet(s) / Function or action | Sign / Nature of the action |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| First House: personality, self-expression | Mars: drive to action | Aries: initiative, impulse |
| Second House: the sensory world, possessions | Venus: the desire to possess | Taurus: beauty, acquisition |
| Third House: mental ability, communication | Mercury: the need to know | Gemini: curiosity, versatility |
| Fourth House: the home, family and protection | Moon: receptiveness, nourishment | Cancer: emotions, closeness |
| Fifth House: creativity, love and enthusiasm | Sun: the life spirit or drive to succeed | Leo: generosity, leadership |
| Sixth House: work, efficiency and health | Mercury: the need to perfect | Virgo: discrimination, analysis, service |
| Seventh House: relationships, partnerships | Venus: the desire to interact | Libra: harmony, arbitration, diplomacy |
| Eighth House: shared senses, self-knowledge | Mars/Pluto: the drive for power | Scorpio: passion, self-mastery |
| Ninth House: knowledge, beliefs and ethics | Jupiter: growth, the desire to expand | Sagittarius: optimism, expansiveness |
| Tenth House: achievement in the world | Saturn: control and application | Capricorn: practicality caution, ambition |
| Eleventh House: friends and the community | Saturn/Uranus: the need to reshape | Aquarius: originality, rebellion, humanity |
| Twelfth House: spiritual and mystic dimensions | Jupiter/Neptune: spiritual growth | Pisces: sympathy, sensitivity, imagination |
