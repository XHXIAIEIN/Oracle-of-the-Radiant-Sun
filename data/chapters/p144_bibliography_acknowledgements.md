# Authors' Acknowledgements

With many thanks to the team at Eddison Sadd, especially Liz Wheeler who has borne indefatigably the brunt of our creative whims, demands and insistences over the years. This book and deck is dedicated to the thousands of nameless artist engravers and masters of the burin who made the world of books pictorial, centuries before the invention of the camera and color printing. Their work still has the originality and power that inspired the collages of artists such as surrealist Max Ernst and many creators amongst the Futurists and Dadaists of the 20s and 30s. We also dedicate this project to Dover Books who have gathered together and published so many of these wonderful and inspiring collections.

The authors' website can be found at www.elementaltarot.com
