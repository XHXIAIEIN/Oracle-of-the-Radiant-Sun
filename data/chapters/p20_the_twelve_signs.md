# The Twelve Signs

The twelve signs of the astrological zodiac also have familiar traditional associations. There follows a brief profile of each sign, detailing the basic nature, the energy, behavior patterns and the negative qualities familiarly associated with each one.

## First Sign: Aries

- **Dates:** 21 March – 20 April
- **Element:** Fire
- **Planetary ruler:** Mars
- **Gemstone:** Diamond

Aries, in its most positive sense, initiates activities, bringing an atmosphere of freshness and newness, or renewal, into the area in which it has influence. It has a pioneering energy, and is courageous and bold, making decisions instantly and without fear for the consequences. Arian behavior is impulsive and often reckless. This sign can be egotistical, impulsive, impatient, aggressive and lacking in subtlety.

## Second Sign: Taurus

- **Dates:** 21 April – 21 May
- **Element:** Earth
- **Planetary ruler:** Venus
- **Gemstone:** Emerald

Taurus is a down-to-earth, practical and plodding sign, artistically talented and proving its value with each step forward. There is an innate sense of the value of material things inherent in this sign. Stable and enduring, Taurean values are strong, unyielding, earthy, and acquisitive with strong desires. Negatively, Taurus can be stick-in-the-mud, stubborn, overly possessive and self-indulgent.

## Third Sign: Gemini

- **Dates:** 22 May – 21 June
- **Element:** Air
- **Planetary ruler:** Mercury
- **Gemstone:** Aquamarine

Gemini's nature is as mercurial as the planet by which it is ruled, displaying a natural inquisitive curiosity to know and see everything. The Gemini mind is agile, versatile, flowing, conversational, airy and packed with ideas. Gemini's ability to communicate with and understand others makes for great social aptitude. Negatively, the Gemini nature is moody and can be volatile, superficial, changeable, restless and inconsistent.

## Fourth Sign: Cancer

- **Dates:** 22 June – 22 July
- **Element:** Water
- **Planetary ruler:** Moon
- **Gemstones:** Pearl, Crystal

Cancer is the maternal sign, sensitive to the needs of others. Usually blessed with a profound memory and a love of tradition and the past, Cancer enjoys a nurturing, protective, tenacious role, and is emotionally sensitive and watery. Family and home are of great importance to the Cancerian nature, which maintains family customs and passes them on to future generations. Negatively, Cancer can be overly protective, unwilling to let go, timid and reclusive.

## Fifth Sign: Leo

- **Dates:** 23 July – 23 August
- **Element:** Fire
- **Planetary ruler:** Sun
- **Gemstone:** Cat's Eye

Leo is the sign of confidence and natural-born leaders. Achievement and appreciation is of great importance to the Leo nature, and this naturally always puts them on show. The lion is generous, warm-hearted, powerful and dramatic, usually with a love of display, and often a splendor and largeness that makes others sit up and take notice. There is great dignity in the lion, but negatively, this sign can be overly proud, vain, extravagant and even arrogant.

## Sixth Sign: Virgo

- **Dates:** 24 August – 22 September
- **Element:** Earth
- **Planetary ruler:** Mercury
- **Gemstones:** Pink Jasper and Hyacinth

Virgo is meticulous, desiring everything to be in order, and hating muddle and inefficiency. Virgo has great discrimination in matter of taste, though always with modesty and simplicity. This sign is often health-conscious, practical and hard-working, and usually has a talent for analysis and a gift for sifting the essence from collections of facts and figures. Negatively, Virgo can display a hyper-critical, petty and perfectionist attitude.

## Seventh Sign: Libra

- **Dates:** 23 September – 23 October
- **Element:** Air
- **Planetary ruler:** Venus
- **Gemstone:** Opal

Libra is balanced and diplomatic, with an ability always to see both sides of a situation. The Libran nature is even-handed, harmonious, artistic, diplomatic and displays a strong sense of fairness. The other side of the coin to a balanced judgment is, of course, a difficulty in coming down on one side or another with regard to important decisions. This character trait can cause Libra to become over-compromising, to the point of giving in for the sake of a quiet life.

## Eighth Sign: Scorpio

- **Dates:** 24 October – 22 November
- **Element:** Water
- **Planetary ruler:** Pluto
- **Gemstone:** Topaz

Scorpio is a powerful and penetrating sign, forever on a quest for the secrets of the deep and unfathomable profundities of life. The Scorpio nature has an enormous capacity for tackling formidable challenges, and in fact this becomes a necessity of life for this deep, brooding sign. Scorpio has a magnetism and an ability to show others what is to come. This sign has an intense, penetrating perception and the power to confront. Negatively, it can be destructive, vengeful, jealous and overly dramatic.

## Ninth Sign: Sagittarius

- **Dates:** 23 November – 21 December
- **Element:** Fire
- **Planetary ruler:** Jupiter
- **Gemstone:** Turquoise

Sagittarius values honesty, straightforwardness and freedom – for themselves and for others also, making Sagittarians say what they think and hang the consequences. Not necessarily too diplomatic, this sign is nonetheless rarely hypocritical. Energetic and outgoing with a good sporty sense of fun and adventure – if life is a journey, Sagittarius is in. Negatively, the sign can be reckless, unrestrained and, above all, tactless.

## Tenth Sign: Capricorn

- **Dates:** 22 December – 20 January
- **Element:** Earth
- **Planetary ruler:** Saturn
- **Gemstone:** Garnet

Capricorn has natural ability as a conscientious and ambitious organizer, and puts its excellent intuitive faculties to good use in achieving every aim in life. The Capricorn mode is slow, steady advancement, solidly based and security-oriented. It is easy for Capricorn to obtain a responsible position in the community, for here, undoubtedly, this sign's abilities lie. Capricorn is disciplined, responsible and reliable, but, negatively, can be pessimistic, overly conventional, rigid, materialistic and callous.

## Eleventh Sign: Aquarius

- **Dates:** 21 January – 18 February
- **Element:** Air
- **Planetary ruler:** Uranus
- **Gemstone:** Sapphire

Aquarius can be defined as an unusual, determined and sometimes stubborn temperament. Not too concerned at what others think about Aquarian beliefs and behavior, this sign can well be considered original if not downright "eccentric." Questioning old "stick-in-the-mud" ways and exploring the new is very much what Aquarius is about. This sign gathers many friends and acquaintances of all shapes, sizes, creeds and colors. Negatively, Aquarius can be rebellious, eccentric, aloof, emotionally superficial and overly extroverted.

## Twelfth Sign: Pisces

- **Dates:** 19 February – 20 March
- **Element:** Water
- **Planetary ruler:** Neptune
- **Gemstone:** Aquamarine

Pisces is extremely sensitive to others' thoughts and feelings. In many ways, this sympathy and feeling for others can easily seduce the sign into becoming and doing what others want. However, the Pisces nature sees success in the pop-star, designer or film-maker who understands intuitively how to reflect the mores of the moment. Pisces is one of the most imaginative zodiac signs. The Pisces nature is compassionate, sensitive, self-sacrificing, gentle and intuitive, but, more negatively, can be prone to escapism, and may be impractical, hyper-sensitive and gullible.
