# Mercury — The Suit of Change

> *Suit title page (p54).* The Mercury glyph (☿︎) above the title "MERCURY — The Suit of Change", with an illustration of a figure driving a chariot drawn by a bird; the signs of Virgo (♍︎) and Gemini (♊︎) — both ruled by Mercury — are shown on the chariot wheels.

By interpreting the combinations of planets and signs found in this suit of cards, you can show how someone reacts to change and intellectual stimulation. The natural place for the planet Mercury in the zodiac wheel is in the Third House, which governs learning and communication. Mercury is associated with the intellect, common sense, reasoning, commerce and business, and its essential qualities are mental activity and versatility. Assessing these cards tells us about how we think, and the way in which we express thoughts and ideas. They show how we communicate, and sum up our learning habits. The Change cards also shed light on how we like to travel through life and how easily we adapt to new life situations, ranging from a fast and adventurous reponse to a slow but sure progression.

*Mercury occupations:* Mathematicians, erudite people, teachers, speakers; secretaries, journalists, editors; merchants, travelers; thieves.

*Mercury affinities:* Wednesday; quicksilver; books, tickets, documents; the nervous system, hands; printing, the media; dogs, monkeys, talking birds; small vehicles.
