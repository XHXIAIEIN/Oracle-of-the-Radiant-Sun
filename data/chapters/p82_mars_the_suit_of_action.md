# Mars — The Suit of Action

> *Suit title page (p82).* The Mars glyph (♂︎) above the title "MARS — The Suit of Action", with an illustration of an armored warrior driving a chariot drawn by two beasts; the signs of Scorpio (♏︎) and Aries (♈︎) — both ruled by Mars — are shown on the chariot wheels.

The Mars cards represent the planet moving through the twelve signs of the astrological zodiac. By interpreting the combinations of planets and signs found in this suit of cards, you can explore where and in what manner the subject of a reading will have to take action in the present circumstances. Mars is the planet of assertion and drive. It tells us about what motivates us and what desires push us into doing the things we do. Mars indicates how we project ourselves, and colors the way in which we perform every task. It also adds information to the nature of the other planets in a reading, the Mars sign combination indicating whether the energies expressed are predominantly physical (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius), material (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn), mental (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) or emotional (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces).

*Mars occupations:* Surgeons, carpenters, mechanics; military personnel; chemists; butchers and farmers.

*Mars affinities:* Tuesday; steel, iron; knives, weapons; red hair, the veins; wars, battles; metal objects, machinery, motors; birds of prey, dogs; fires, fireplaces.
