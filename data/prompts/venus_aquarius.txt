Card-specific visual facts:
- Subject: an intelligent person's detached head floats before a watery landscape, marked with a phrenological map.
- Body orientation: only the head and neck are visible, front-facing and centered low in the composition.
- Face and gaze: the face looks straight ahead with calm, distant interest, emotionally reserved and absorbed in thought.
- Hands and gestures: no hands appear; stillness and mental focus replace physical action.
- Clothing and props: the cranium is labeled with numbered intellect areas, making the head itself the central prop.
- Environment: a sea or wide inlet stretches behind the head, with islands, trees, boats, and a distant town.
- Background decoration: sailboats and shoreline architecture form an idyllic scene that the thinker seems unaware of.
- Spatial mood: the close head blocks much of the water view, separating intellect from the emotional sea behind it.
