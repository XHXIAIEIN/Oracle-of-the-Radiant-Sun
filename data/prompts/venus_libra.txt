Card-specific visual facts:
- Subject: Libra's indecision appears between a gentle virgin constellation figure and the dramatic sting of Scorpio.
- Body orientation: the pale winged figure floats on the left while a darker scorpion-like figure stands partly hidden at right.
- Face and gaze: the left figure looks calmly outward; the right figure is obscured, adding secrecy and tension.
- Hands and gestures: the left figure holds a slender stem or wand, while the right raises arms near the head.
- Clothing and props: a soft yellow robe with star markings contrasts with the dark silhouetted figure by the tree.
- Environment: a large tree divides the composition, creating a visual fork between open air and shadowed foliage.
- Background decoration: an hourglass with wings sits below, explicitly marking time and the pressure to decide.
- Spatial mood: the image balances light and dark on either side of the tree, making hesitation feel suspended but urgent.
